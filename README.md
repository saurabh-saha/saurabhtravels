# saurabh.travels 🧭

Personal road-trip travelogue — roads, dogs & thinking.

Seven drives across India in a Hyundai i20 automatic, with a rotating crew of
wife, Cooper the golden retriever, Mom & Dad, Aryan, and Hrithik.

A single-file site (`index.html`, no build step, no dependencies) with:

- Airbnb-inspired design — card grid, filters by year & people, listing-style trip pages
- Animated SVG route maps — the car actually drives every leg, cities light up as it passes
- Full-trip playback by default, with per-leg zoom & highlight

## Run it

Open `index.html` in a browser. That's it.
